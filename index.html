Welcome to on, a trusted name in A.F FABRICATION WORKS machines services and products.

Established in 2007, A.F FABRICATION WORKS started as a small fabrication unit focused on quality and reliability. With consistent innovation, we grew into a trusted manufacturer of packing, conveying, drying, and coating machines for the food, dairy, and industrial sectors. Today, we proudly serve clients across India with 18+ years of engineering excellence and proven performance.

Our mission is to deliver high-performance, durable, and cost-effective machines that simplify industrial operations and boost productivity. We aim to combine innovation, quality fabrication, and customer satisfaction to set new standards in packaging and material-handling solutions.p>

Our vision is to become a leading name in industrial machinery manufacturing, known for innovation, precision, and long-lasting performance. We strive to build machines that empower industries, improve efficiency, and contribute to a smarter, more automated future.
